### Explanation of Services

1. **Authentication Service**:
   - **GoogleAuthService**: Handles user authentication via Google accounts and retrieves user profile information.

2. **Template Service**:
   - **TemplateService**: Provides CSV templates for different chart types.

3. **Upload Service**:
   - **UploadService**: Manages the upload of CSV files and initiates validation.

4. **Validation Service**:
   - **ValidationService**: Validates the uploaded CSV files against the template structure.

5. **Chart Generation Service**:
   - **ChartGenerationService**: Generates charts using the data from the CSV files and saves them in multiple formats.

6. **Dashboard Service**:
   - **DashboardService**: Displays the history of user-generated charts and allows downloading of charts.

7. **Quota Service**:
   - **QuotaService**: Manages user quotas, including charging for chart creation and selling additional quotas.

8. **Payment Service**:
   - **PaymentService**: Processes payments for quota purchases.

9. **User Profile Service**:
   - **UserProfileService**: Maintains and updates user profiles.

10. **Notification Service**:
    - **NotificationService**: Sends notifications to users (e.g., quota updates, chart generation status).
