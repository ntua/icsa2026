### Explanation

- **Model Package:**  
  - **CoordinateGroup** represents a coordinate group record with a unique ID, timestamp, label, and both Cartesian and Polar coordinates.  
  - **CartesianCoordinate** and **PolarCoordinate** represent the two coordinate types, and are composed by the CoordinateGroup.

- **DAO Package:**  
  - **DatabaseManager** handles the JDBC connection to the MySQL database.  
  - **CoordinateGroupDAO** provides methods to add, update, delete, and retrieve coordinate group records.

- **Service Package:**  
  - **CoordinateConverter** provides methods to convert between Cartesian and Polar representations.  
  - **CoordinateService** implements the business logic (creation, update, deletion, retrieval, and conversion) and uses the DAO.

- **Controller Package:**  
  - **CoordinateGroupController** acts as an intermediary between the GUI and the business logic.

- **GUI Package:**  
  - **MainFrame** is the main window of the application.  
  - **CoordinateGroupForm** is used for input (creation and modification).  
  - **CoordinateGroupListPanel** displays the list of coordinate groups.

This class diagram meets the requirements and reflects the requested client-server (thick client) architecture with a clear separation of concerns.
