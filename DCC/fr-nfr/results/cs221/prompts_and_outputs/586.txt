  - **DccController** acts as the mediator between the UI, the data access objects, and the logic. It performs operations such as add, update, delete, and filtering coordinate groups.

  - **CoordinateConverter** provides methods to convert between Cartesian and Polar coordinates.
  
- **data Package (Persistence):**
